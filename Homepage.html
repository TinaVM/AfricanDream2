Who Are We

African Dream was established in 2000 to make a difference in economic development of Limpopo. African Dream is a black owned entity, owned entirely (100%) by a woman. Its main speciality is Event Management, with an intense focus on Catering and Equipment Hire.


Target Market

Because we are able to meet the expectations of our intended market, and that our standards compare nationally, our Target Market are:-

  • Corporate
  • Government
  • Parastatals
  • Individuals
  • NGOs
